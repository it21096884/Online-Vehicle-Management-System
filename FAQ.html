Rental Requirements

What´s required when renting a car?

You must be above 25 and have held a valid driver’s license. You must also have a valid passport and an international credit card, issued under your name, with sufficient funds for a credit hold (safety deposit). Additionally, depending on your destination legislation, it´s important to have and hold an International Driving Permit.

Can I add an Additional Driver?

Yes, we do allow up to 10 additional drivers per contract. If the additional drivers cannot be present when you pick up the vehicle, they can be registered with their valid driving license at any other branch (main driver must be present as well). A fee will be applied for adding each additional driver.

Can elderly also rent car?

Yes. No age restrictions are in place. Anybody with a valid driver’s license can rent a vehicle.

For how many days can I reserve a rental car?

We accept reservations for a rental period of one (1) month at most.

Can I request an AT (automatic) or MT (manual) vehicle?

Yes. Both AT (automatic) vehicles and MT (manual) vehicles are available.

Reservation

Can I rent a car without a reservation?

Yes, as long as a vehicle is available in the station; however, we recommend making a reservation because no vehicle may be available during busy periods.

Will there be a problem if the person who made the reservation is different from the one who will drive the car?

No. Persons who will not drive the car can also make the reservation; however, they need to confirm first the driver of the vehicle before making the reservation.

Can I cancel my reservation?

Yes, reservations can be cancelled. Please contact us as soon as possible if you wish to cancel your reservation. The reservation number issued when you made the reservation and the email address you registered at the time of reservation are required to cancel or make changes to your reservation.

Can I make changes to my reservation?

Yes. The reservation number issued when you made the reservation and the email address you registered at the time of reservation are required to make changes to your reservation.

Confirmation & Payment

Can I pay by credit card?

No. Only cash and cheque are accepted.

Can I pay in cash?

Yes. We generally require payment by credit card, but , we allow payment in cash at the rental station where the vehicle is to be returned. If you wish to pay in cash, you must also submit an item of identification (copies are acceptable) in addition to your driver’s license. You will be required to pay by credit card if you rent a car from a rental station without a reservation.

When do I have to pay the rental fees?

Please pay the fees when returned the vehicle at the reservation.

How is the rental fee calculated?

The rental fee is determined in accordance with the model of the vehicle and the length of the rental period and according tho the distance traveled. If the user has requested any additional options, accessories, compensation package, or other services, the applicable costs will be added to the basic fee accordingly.

Insuarance & Protection

Do I need to get the Safety Package or the Super Safety Package?

No. Subscribing to these packages is not mandatory. A package can be selected when making a reservation or when picking up the vehicle.

What happens if I get in an accident and am not subscribed to the Safety Package?

All of our rental vehicles are covered for unlimited body injury compensation (Deductible: LKR 89,000); unlimited property damage compensation (Deductible: LKR 89,000); personal injury compensation up to LKR 356,000; and market-value vehicle damage compensation (Deductible: LKR 89,000 to LKR 178,000). These are included in the rental fee. In the event of an accident, the user will be required to pay the deductible. A non-operation charge (partial compensation for loss of earnings) will be imposed as well.

Does compensation apply to all drivers?

Everyone registered as a driver at the time the vehicle is picked up will be compensated in the same way as the applicant.
